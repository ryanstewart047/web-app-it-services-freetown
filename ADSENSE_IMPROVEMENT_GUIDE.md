### 9. Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Copying content from other websites
❌ Using AI to write entire articles without heavy editing
❌ Short, thin content (under 800 words)
❌ No images or poorly formatted text
❌ Only promotional content about your services
❌ Reapplying too soon (wait at least 2-3 months with improvements)

### 10. What Makes Quality Content?

✅ **Original**: Written in your own words, unique perspective
✅ **Helpful**: Answers real questions your customers have
✅ **Detailed**: Thorough explanations, not surface-level
✅ **Well-formatted**: Headers, bullets, short paragraphs
✅ **Visual**: Images, infographics, screenshots
✅ **Accurate**: Factual, reliable information
✅ **Updated**: Current information, not outdated
✅ **Engaging**: Easy to read, interesting, conversational tone
